GERMANIUM

Symbol: Ge

Atomic Number: 32

Atomic Weight: 72.61

Discovery: Clemens Winkler 1886 (Germany)

Electron Configuration: [Ar] 4s2 3d10 4p2

Element Classification: Semimetallic

Density (g/cc): 5.323

Melting Point (K): 1210.6

Boiling Point (K): 3103

Appearance: grayish-white metal

Isotopes: There are 30 known isotopes of germanium ranging from Ge-60 to Ge-89. 

Atomic Radius (pm): 137

Covalent Radius (pm): 122

Ionic Radius: 53 (+4e) 73 (+2e)

Specific Heat (@20°C J/g mol): 0.322

First Ionizing Energy (kJ/mol): 760.0

Oxidation States: +4 is the most common. +1, +2 and -4 exist but are rare.

Lattice Structure: Diagonal

Lattice Constant (Å): 5.660
