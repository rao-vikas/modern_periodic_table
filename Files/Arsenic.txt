ARSENIC

Symbol: As

Atomic Number: 33

Atomic Weight: 74.92159

Discovery: Albertus Magnus (1250)

Electron Configuration: [Ar] 4s2 3d10 4p3

Element Classification: Semimetallic

Density (g/cc): 5.73
 
Melting Point: 1090 K at 35.8 atmospheres (triple point of arsenic).  At normal pressure, arsenic has no melting point. 

Boiling Point (K): 876

Appearance: steel-grey, brittle semimetal

Isotopes: There are 30 known isotopes of arsenic ranging from As-63 to As-92. Arsenic has one stable isotope: As-75.

Atomic Radius (pm): 139

Covalent Radius (pm): 120

Ionic Radius: 46 (+5e) 222 (-3e)

Specific Heat (@20°C J/g mol): 0.328

Pauling Negativity Number: 2.18

First Ionizing Energy (kJ/mol): 946.2

Oxidation States: 5, 3, -2

Lattice Structure: Rhombohedral

Lattice Constant (Å): 4.130
